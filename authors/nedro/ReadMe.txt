Nedro's BNE-style Puds

The best BNE-style maps offer something that has never been done or something common yet done better than any other map. This is a small pack of maps I created mostly around 2000 but up to 2017. Here are a few notes : 

Party at Midnight BNE - This map is one of the best FFA maps you will ever play because it forces the action. It also plays well 1 vs 1. 

Slivers (both versions) - A Spiral-inspired for up to 7 players. While both versions work well any format, I recommend the classic version for 6+ players and the BNE version for 4 or less.
